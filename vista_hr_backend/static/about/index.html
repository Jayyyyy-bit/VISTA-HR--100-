Our Mission
Housing search,
reimagined
The main objective of VISTA-HR is to provide a centralized web-based platform that digitizes and streamlines the room listing, browsing, and booking processes for boarding houses in Metro Manila. By offering real-time availability updates, virtual room viewing, and automated scheduling, the system aims to enhance operational efficiency for owners while providing tenants with a reliable and convenient rental experience.
